BUSY LIFE

I really enjoy documentary photography so I decided to go for the Busy Life topic where I could shoot some street photography as part of the Busy Life. I then finalised my type of street photography i wanted to shoot which was people beeing so busy on their phones, they do not even recognise the life around them. This was a appeal to myself and everyone to realise how often we stare at our phone walking through the city, not paying attention to anything or anyone. It has become so normal to look at our phone, we do not realise how perfidious it has become.
